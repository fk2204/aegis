## Commercial financing disclosure

**SB 2234 (2023)** — Illinois Commercial Financing Disclosure Act.
Requires APR-equivalent disclosure for commercial financing
transactions UNDER $500,000. Small business loans (term loan,
revolving credit, line of credit) require an annualized APR
disclosure at the offer stage.

* **Bill:** SB 2234, 102nd General Assembly, signed 2023
* **Effective:** delayed implementation per IDFPR rulemaking
* **AEGIS impact:** none at the broker layer — funder generates
  the disclosure under their own license.

## MCA-specific framework

No MCA-specific licensing or disclosure law in IL as of
2026-06-30. General commercial lending statutes apply. The IDFPR
(Illinois Department of Financial and Professional Regulation)
has not issued MCA-specific rules.
