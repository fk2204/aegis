## Home-state context

Wyoming is **Commera Capital's home state of incorporation**.
Commera is registered as a Wyoming LLC; AEGIS is the internal
underwriting tool. The Wyoming Secretary of State is the
authoritative source for Commera's good-standing status.

## MCA-specific framework

No MCA-specific licensing or disclosure law in Wyoming as of
2026-06-30. General Wyoming commercial lending statutes apply
(Wyo. Stat. § 13-2 et seq. for state-chartered lenders, but
those do not extend to commercial financing or MCA).

The Wyoming Division of Banking has not issued MCA-specific
rules. State-side MCA activity is unregulated at the broker
layer.

## Operator awareness

* No registration requirement for AEGIS / Commera as an ISO broker
* No state-mandated disclosure form
* Annual Wyoming franchise tax / annual report due via SOS
  (administrative — confirm Commera filing current)
