# CA — Statutes and citations

**Statute:** SB 1235 (2018), amended by SB 362 (2025); 10 CCR § 950 et seq.

**Effective:** 2022-12-09 (regs); SB 362 effective 2026-01-01.

**Scope:** All commercial financing ≤ $500,000.

**APR:** Required; calculated via Reg Z § 1026.22 actuarial method on the actual payment stream.

**Broker registration:** No state broker registration; provider needs a CFL license.

**Penalties:** DFPI civil enforcement + restitution. No private right of action.

**Notes:** SB 362 limits use of "rate"/"interest" terminology in marketing and triggers renewal re-disclosure.
