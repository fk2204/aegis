# FL — Statutes and citations

**Statute:** HB 1353 (Florida Commercial Financing Disclosure Law, FCFDL); Fla. Stat. § 559.961 et seq.

**Effective:** 2024-01-01.

**Scope:** All commercial financing ≤ $500,000 (loans, OEC, AR purchase, MCA).

**APR:** Not required.

**Broker registration:** No state broker registration required.

**Penalties:** $500–$20,000 per violation; up to $50,000 for continued violations. Florida AG enforces. No PRA.

**Notes:** COJ banned outright. **Broker advance fees prohibited (Fla. Stat. § 559.9614(1)(a))** — narrow carve-out for actual third-party costs paid by check/MO to an independent third party. Broker advertising must include address and phone.
