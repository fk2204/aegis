## Commercial financing disclosure

**P.L. 2023, c.74** — New Jersey Commercial Financing Disclosure Law.
Effective 2024. Requires APR-equivalent disclosure for commercial
financing transactions, plus broker fee disclosure where a broker
is involved.

* **Statute:** N.J.S.A. 17:16BB-1 et seq.
* **Disclosure scope:** APR-equivalent, total cost of capital,
  broker compensation, finance charge, payment schedule
* **AEGIS impact:** broker fee disclosure may touch the broker
  layer when AEGIS / Commera is the named broker. Funder still
  owns the borrower-facing CFDL disclosure under their own
  license. Confirm with the funder partnership which side
  generates the disclosure for NJ deals.
