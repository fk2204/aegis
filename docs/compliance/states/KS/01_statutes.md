# KS — Statutes and citations

**Statute:** SB 345 (2024); Kansas Commercial Financing Disclosure Act.

**Effective:** 2024-07-01.

**Scope:** Commercial financing ≤ $500,000 (loans, OEC, lines of credit, AR purchase).

**APR:** Not required.

**Broker registration:** No state broker registration required.

**Penalties:** Standard civil penalties; no MCA-specific cap published. Kansas AG enforces. TODO: verify cap with KS AG opinion or implementing regulation.

**Notes:** Standard disclosures; merchant signature required. COJ void by general law.
