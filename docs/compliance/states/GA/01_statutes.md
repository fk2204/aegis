# GA — Statutes and citations

**Statute:** SB 90 (2023); OCGA § 10-1-393.18 et seq. (amends Georgia FBPA).

**Effective:** 2024-01-01.

**Scope:** Commercial financing ≤ $500,000 (loans, AR purchase; sales-based covered).

**APR:** Not required (actuarial Reg Z method optional).

**Broker registration:** No state broker registration required.

**Penalties:** $500–$20,000 per violation; up to $50,000 for continued conduct. Georgia AG enforces. **NO PRIVATE RIGHT OF ACTION.**

**Notes:** **Broker advance fees prohibited (OCGA § 10-1-393.20).** Forum venue restricted via OCGA § 9-12-18. No deceptive representations about the financing's character.
