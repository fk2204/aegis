# VA — Statutes and citations

**Statute:** HB 1027 (2022); VA Sales-Based Financing Disclosure Act.

**Effective:** 2022-07-01.

**Scope:** MCA only (sales-based) ≤ $500,000.

**APR:** Not required.

**Broker registration:** Required — VA SCC, annual renewal.

**Penalties:** Up to $50,000 per deal. VA SCC enforces. No PRA.

**Notes:** Forum-selection clauses naming non-VA venue are unenforceable. Arbitration cannot require face-to-face proceedings outside merchant's principal place of business; provider pays arbitration costs. **COJ in MCA contracts is void.**
