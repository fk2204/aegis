## MCA registration

**Act 2022-80** — Pennsylvania commercial financing registration.
MCA providers and certain commercial financing brokers must
register with the **PA Department of Banking and Securities (DOBS)**
and provide disclosure to recipients.

* **Bill:** Act 80 of 2022 (HB 1971)
* **Effective:** registration window opened 2023
* **Disclosure:** APR-equivalent + total cost of capital required
* **AEGIS impact:** none at the broker layer — funder registers
  under their own corporate identity. Confirm the funder
  partnership carries valid PA DOBS registration before submitting
  a PA-state merchant.
