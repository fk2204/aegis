# CT — Statutes and citations

**Statute:** SB 1032 (2023); CT Sales-Based Financing Disclosure Act.

**Effective:** 2024-07-01 (no-action posture through 2024-10-01 per CT DOB guidance).

**Scope:** MCA only (sales-based) ≤ $250,000.

**APR:** Not required.

**Broker registration:** Required — CT DOB, annual renewal by October 1.

**Penalties:** Up to $100,000 per violation. CT DOB enforces. No PRA.

**Notes:** Broker compensation disclosure required. Double-dipping disclosure on renewal. Renewal re-disclosure required. COJ void by general law.
