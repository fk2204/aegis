# LA — Statutes and citations

**Statute:** HB 470 (2025); Louisiana commercial financing disclosure law.

**Effective:** 2025-08-01.

**Scope:** MCA only (sales-based). **NO DEAL-SIZE CAP.** No entity exemptions, no bank carve-out.

**APR:** Not required.

**Broker registration:** No state broker registration required.

**Penalties:** $500–$50,000 per violation. Louisiana AG enforces. **NO PRIVATE RIGHT OF ACTION.**

**Notes:** 6 disclosure fields required. AG-only enforcement is structural — no PRA. COJ permitted in commercial.
