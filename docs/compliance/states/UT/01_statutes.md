# UT — Statutes and citations

**Statute:** HB 198 (2022); UT Commercial Financing Registration and Disclosure Act.

**Effective:** 2023-01-01.

**Scope:** All commercial financing ≤ $1,000,000.

**APR:** Not required.

**Broker registration:** Required — UT DFI via NMLS, annual renewal.

**Penalties:** Civil penalty up to $20,000. UT DFI enforces. No PRA.

**Notes:** Provider must disclose funds paid to brokers on the deal.
