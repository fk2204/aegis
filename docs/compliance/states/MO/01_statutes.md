# MO — Statutes and citations

**Statute:** SB 1359; § 427.300 RSMo.

**Effective:** 2025-02-28.

**Scope:** Standard commercial financing ≤ $500,000.

**APR:** Not required.

**Broker registration:** **UNUSUAL** — brokers register with MO Division of Finance (one-time + maintenance). Providers do NOT register.

**Penalties:** Missouri AG civil enforcement. No MCA-specific cap published. No PRA.

**Notes:** Broker-only registration is the unique feature; providers proceed without registration but must comply with disclosure obligations.
