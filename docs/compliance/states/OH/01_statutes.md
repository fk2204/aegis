## MCA-specific framework

No MCA-specific licensing or disclosure law in Ohio as of
2026-06-30. General commercial lending statutes apply (Ohio Rev.
Code Chapter 1321 — small loans; not MCA-specific).

The Ohio Division of Financial Institutions (DFI) has not issued
MCA-specific rules. ORC § 1321 regulates licensed small-loan
lenders but does not apply to commercial financing or MCA
arrangements at the broker layer.

## Operator awareness

* No registration requirement for AEGIS / Commera as an ISO broker
* No state-mandated disclosure form at the broker layer
* General consumer-protection and TCPA / state UDAP rules still
  apply to broker conduct
