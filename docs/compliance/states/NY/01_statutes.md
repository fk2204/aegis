# NY — Statutes and citations

**Statute:** CFDL — SB 5470 + S898 (2020, amended 2021); Financial Services Law Article 8 + 23 NYCRR Part 600.

**Effective:** 2023-08-01 (mandatory compliance).

**Scope:** All commercial financing ≤ $2,500,000. Broad product scope.

**APR:** Required; Reg Z § 1026.22 or merchant-opt-in alternative.

**Broker registration:** No state broker registration required.

**Penalties:** $2,000 per violation; $10,000 per intentional violation. NY DFS enforces. No PRA.

**Notes:** Broker compensation in writing (23 NYCRR § 600.21(f)). Renewal re-disclosure § 600.17. 4-year retention. AG enforcement risk HIGH (2025 Yellowstone $1.065B).
